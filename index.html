About Megan

Not your basic bookkeeper.

I've been living in the world of numbers and businesses for over 20 years - and I mean that literally. At 17, I was already working for a small IT company, building computers, setting up networks, and learning QuickBooks Desktop before I knew what a balance sheet was. That dual fluency in technology and finance has followed me ever since.

Over the next two decades I worked my way through office management, accounting, and bookkeeping across multiple industries - including years inside a CPA firm where I watched small business owners not receive the dedication and attention they deserved, yet were still billed for it.

That's why I started Muse Bookkeeping in January 2020. Not because bookkeeping needed another service provider - but because small business owners deserved better. Clear communication, clean books delivered on time, and a bookkeeper who maintains consistent communication and actually explains what the numbers mean.

Over the years I've had the pleasure of working with consultants, coaches, small marketing agencies, a video game developer, manufacturing companies, and an aircraft MRO.

I fully understand the inner workings of service-based businesses - from their usual tech stack, chart of accounts, and communication styles. I also still have my soft spot for IT/MSPs - I understand your RMM and why you have RMR, your contractor payments, and your tool stack. You won't spend twenty minutes explaining how your business works.

And please tell me you're not still billing by the hour and wasting hours on invoicing!

Every engagement is custom quoted. Pricing is based on the specific combination of services your business needs, including transaction volume, number of accounts, adjusting entries, deferred revenue schedules, prepaid expenses, payroll accruals, subcontractor accruals, tool stack reconciliation, COGS mapping, labor allocation to COGS, franchise reporting, multi-territory tracking, deadline guarantees, and any cleanup or catch-up work required. No two businesses are the same, so no two quotes are the same. Schedule a free consultation and we will tell you exactly what it will cost.

We start with a discovery call to learn about your business and what you need. From there we gather the details to build your custom quote. Once you are ready, we send over an Engagement Letter that lays out everything in plain language. Sign it as-is or come back with questions. After signing, you receive a payment authorization form. You are auto-billed a flat monthly fee on the first of every month. We then begin onboarding. Once that is completed, my team and I get to work.

Reconciled books, a profit and loss statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement delivered by the 15th of every month. Expedited financials by the 10th are available upon request. We schedule a one hour monthly call to go over everything with you. You also get unlimited texts and emails with a 24 hour response time.
