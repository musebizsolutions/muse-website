About Muse

Not your basic bookkeeper.

20+ years of accounting experience. Deep roots in IT and MSPs. A firm founded because small business owners deserved better.

Megan Muse

Founder & Lead Bookkeeper

I've been living in the world of numbers and businesses for over 20 years. At 17, I was already working for a small IT company, building computers, setting up networks, and learning QuickBooks Desktop before I knew what a balance sheet was.

Over the next two decades I worked through office management, accounting, and bookkeeping across multiple industries - including years inside a CPA firm where I watched small business owners not receive the dedication and attention they deserved, yet were still billed for it.

That's why I started Muse Bookkeeping in January 2020. Not because bookkeeping needed another service provider - but because small business owners deserved better. Clear communication, clean books delivered on time, and a bookkeeper who actually explains what the numbers mean.

I fully understand the inner workings of IT/MSPs - your RMM and why you have RMR, your contractor payments, and your tool stack. You won't spend twenty minutes explaining how your business works.
